Volunteer Work

For the last 10 years I have been a volunteer at LINK (Lawrence Interdenominational Kitchen) in Lawrence Ks. LINK serves warm nutritious meals free of charge four times a week. On top of being a regular server at LINK, I'm also the Secretary for the board. As a board member I regularly plan events, help with fundraising, help maintain the location of LINK, and assist with any issues that may come up in our day to day operations.
